Welcome to Finch

This quick setup wizard will help you configure everything you need to get started. We'll import your first bank CSV, match up the column headers, identify your pay day, and optionally set up merchant logo enrichment.

The whole process takes about two minutes and you can skip any step at any time.

Import your bank CSV

Upload a CSV export from your bank. Finch will detect the column headers and help you map them to the right fields.

Identify your pay day

Finch uses a keyword from your salary or main income transaction to detect pay periods. Click on the transaction that represents your pay day — then trim the keyword if needed.

Other income sources

Select any other transactions that represent income — dividends, freelance payments, benefits, refunds, etc. These won't be counted as spending. Tick as many as apply, or skip if your pay day keyword covers everything.

Merchant logos

Finch can show merchant logos next to your transactions using the logo.dev API. This is optional — you can configure it later in Settings.

Create a free account at logo.dev to get your API keys. The publishable key is used to display logos; the secret key enables brand search.

Keys are stored locally in your Finch database and never sent to any third party other than logo.dev.

You're all set!
