## Analysis — Spending

Category breakdown table with amounts, percentages, and transaction counts. Spending-by-tag breakdown on the right.

## Analysis — Subscriptions

Full subscription list grouped by category, with monthly cost, last-seen date, and paid/N/A/overdue status. Monthly and annual cost summary cards at the top.



---

## Analysis — Runway

Days-until-payday tracker, where-you-stand breakdown, daily allowance calculation, and subscription checklist for the current period.



---

## Transactions

Paginated transaction list with filters for account, category, and period. Shows merchant logos, transaction descriptions, amounts, and assigned categories.

## Rules

Categorisation rules list showing keywords, target categories, priority, and subscription flags. Rules are applied automatically on CSV import.



---

## Categories

Hierarchical category list with parent and sub-categories, custom colours, and rule counts.



---

## Settings

Configuration page showing pay day/income keyword setup, CSV format and column mapping, currency symbol, logo.dev API keys, and database export/import.
